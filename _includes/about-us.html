This year, we have decided to host a hackathon in honour of the English Mathematician Ada Lovelace, AdaHack. The hackathon aims to celebrate our communityâ€™s creativity and ideas and is a great opportunity for students from different backgrounds to work together in teams and come up with innovative solutions to real-world problems. It is a 48 hour virtual hackathon in collaboration with Major League Hacking open to gender minorities across Scotland. The main medium of communication and announcements during the hackathon will be Discord. Students can form teams of up to 4 people to submit their projects via Devpost. There are a lot of exciting challenges and prizes for students of all programming capabilities.

The Edinburgh Hoppers is the official society for gender minorities in the School of Informatics at the University of Edinburgh. The objective of the society is to provide a safe space for students of gender minorities to network and enhance their skills.
