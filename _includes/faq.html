Frequently asked questions

Q: I am a postgraduate student, can I participate?

A: Yes you can ! The hackathon is open to all students of The University of Edinburgh.

Q: Is the hackathon beginner- friendly?

A: Absolutely! We encourage beginners to participate as this is valuable learning experience

Q: I’ll be busy on the first day of the hackathon, can I join later on ?

A: Yes. You can use the hackathon’s discord channel to form teams.

Q: I don’t have a team, can I join?

A: Yes! We will have a discord channel with the sole purpose of helping you find teamsmates, that will be active through out the hackathon. You can also submit an individual project
